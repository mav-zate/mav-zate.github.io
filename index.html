About Me

I am a New York based web developer experienced in JavaScript, Ruby, Java, Python, HTML, and CSS with extensive knowledge of the React.js and Ruby on Rails frameworks. Having completed an undergraduate degree in mathematics, I am able to appreciate the theoretical foundations of computer systems and use them to my advantage to write elegant and efficient code. There are few feelings greater than publishing clear, reliable, and adaptable code.

I'm experienced in web development, game design, and language technologies, and am seeking challenging opportunities that will expand my skill as an engineer.

When I am not coding, you can usually find me skating, reading Arabic literature, keeping up with linguistics research, and playing Overwatch with friends.
